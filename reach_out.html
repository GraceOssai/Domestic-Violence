HOW WE HELP

We think that girls' voices can drive change, not just in the future but also in the here and now.
Our programming strategy gives young women all around the world the power to make
the choices that will affect their lives and the communities in which they live.

1

Shielding the girl child

"Shielding" refers to the measures we take to stop or prevent the abuse of children and young people, including empowering staff to report and address concerns in order to make everyone safer.

2

Surveillance and Assessment

To make sure that our initiatives to support girls in these areas are efficient, significant, and accountable, we strive to set up monitoring and evaluation mechanisms in communities where these behaviors are widespread.

3

Awareness

We put up campaigns to educate people and include entire communities, both online and off, with an emphasis on human rights, gender equality, sexual education, and the needs of women and girls who experience its effects.
